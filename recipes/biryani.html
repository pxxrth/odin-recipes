Biryani

Home

Description

Biryani is a famous south asian dish generally served with chicken but also has variations with other meats and also potato.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Tikka Masala
  • Green Chilies

Steps

  1. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes
  2. Boil the rice until it is 70% cooked
  3. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and tikka masala
  4. Fry the onions until golden brown and add tomato paste
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is 70% cooked
  6. Layer the rice and chicken in a pot and cook for about 10 mins until the chicken is fully cooked
