Fried Potatoes with Vermicelli

Home

Description

Fried Potatoes with Vermicelli is a simple dish that is easy to make and is a great way to use up leftover potatoes. It is a popular dish in many parts of the world and is often served as a side dish or as a main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 Potatos
  • Any type of oil
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Coriander Powder
  • 1 cup Vermicelli
