Fried Potatoes with Vermicelli

Home

Description

Fried Potatoes with Vermicelli is a simple dish that is easy to make and is a great way to use up leftover potatoes. It is a popular dish in many parts of the world and is often served as a side dish or as a main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 Potatos
  • Any type of oil
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Coriander Powder
  • 1 cup Vermicelli

Steps

Potatoes

  1. Peel the potatoes into dices
  2. Put 2 spoons of oil in the pan
  3. Heat up the oil and add the potatoes
  4. Fry until potatoes are golden brown
    • Add some water if the potatoes too crispy
    • Add some oil if theyre too soft
  5. Add the Red chilli powder and about 2 teaspoons of salt in the potatoes
  6. Add a bit of coriander powder just for the look of the potatoes
  7. stir until fully cooked

Vermicelli

  1. Pour about 3 cups of water in a pot
  2. Add 1.5 spoons of oil and 2 teaspoons of Salt
  3. boil the water completely
  4. Add the Vermicelli in the pot
  5. Let the Vermicelli submerge in the water
  6. Stir the Vermicelli well
  7. Add Cardamom Powder for the aroma in the Vermicelli
  8. Stir it once more to completely mix the Vermicelli with the Cardamom Powder
